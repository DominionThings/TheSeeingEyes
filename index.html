About The Seeing Eyes

Started on 7th December 2014, The Seeing Eyes (TSE) is committed to fighting preventable blindness. We have treated over 50,000 Ugandans and beyond with eye conditions under our community outreach and corporate partnerships. Be apart of this move to have sight recovered in Africa’s hard to reach places

Our Mission

Our mission is to advance eye and vision care through awareness, treatment, and monitoring. We strive hard to provide the utmost care to all the communities by providing visual health services.

Our Vision

To prevent avoidable blindess, especially in the hard to reach areas of Uganda.
